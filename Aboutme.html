About me 

By Adaline Kali Teri posted May 8, 2016

Hello !

So as you may understand, my name is Adaline Kali Teri, I was born in 1998.

I read a lot since I learned words, and I love to write stories about what cares for me in this little big world. 

I also love to draw, to paint, to invent characters with big eyes. I have already many muses like Taylor Momsen, Chrissy Costanza, Cara Delevigne, and i really enjoy people who care about the others.


I'm a big dreamer and every morning, when i woke up, i hope the day will be a big day, with lot of love, and lot of things to learn. 

I love authentic people, I love to talk with them, to dream with them.

I write stories that makes me dream about the world, that change the world we know for a better one without fear, hate and all the stuff that make me and other living creatures suffer. 

I draw because i have to create things, i have to make my mind on the paper in an other way that when I write.

I hope you like my work !
